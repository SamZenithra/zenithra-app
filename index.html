FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from the Profile page under Manage Subscription, or by emailing [email protected]. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
Will I be charged during my free trial?
No. Your 28-day free trial gives you full access to all features with no payment taken. You only get charged if you choose to subscribe after the trial ends.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing date.
Is my data safe?
Yes. We use encrypted connections and secure cloud storage. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
I forgot my password — what do I do?
Tap "Forgot password?" on the login screen and enter your email. We'll send a reset link immediately.
My data disappeared — can it be recovered?
Currently the app stores data locally on your device. If you cleared your browser data or reinstalled, data may be lost. Cloud sync is coming in a future update. Contact us at [email protected] if you need help.
The app is not working correctly
Try refreshing the page or restarting the app. If the problem persists, email [email protected] with a description of the issue and your device type.
How is BMI calculated?
BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height (m)². It is a general indicator only and does not account for muscle mass, age, or ethnicity. Always consult a healthcare professional for personalised advice.
How is my fasting calorie contribution estimated?
Zenithra estimates calorie burn from fasting based on your selected fasting window length, using published research averages. These are estimates only — individual results vary.
